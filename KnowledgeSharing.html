Portio

This project explores the possibility to incorporate interaction design (IxD) to empathize and understand the context of knowledge sharing, with the goal to create a suitable platform for it. Therefore, there was a heavy focus on applying user experience, usability, and user-centered design to the final product. To accomplish this, specific IxD methods are selected and used throughout the design process, which in this project was The Triple Diamond. Below, images from the design process can be seen.
