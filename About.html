About Me

I am a dynamic and capable UX Design Engineer with an international background and an eye for detail while maintaining a holistic focus.

I hold a Master of Interaction Design and Technologies and a Bachelor of Industrial Design Engineering, both from Chalmers University of Technology. Additionally, I studied at the University of Porto, Faculty of Engineering, in their Master of Multimedia program during the fall of 2023. My studies have included UX/UI, user-centered design, user testing, A/B testing, human research, and product development, alongside requisite courses for an engineering degree, including advanced mathematics and physics. During this time, I have become highly proficient in Figma and Adobe Suite, in addition to a working knowledge of front-end programming languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

While I was born in the United States, I spent my childhood in Sweden before moving to the multicultural city of Toronto for High School. Holding Swedish, Canadian, and American citizenships, I offer a unique perspective for any type of job or opportunity. Living across continents has imparted an instinct to connect, communicate, and create strong bonds. This international upbringing is a strength that I leverage daily in my interactions, both personally and professionally.
