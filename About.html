About Me

My name is Mats Kullerstrand and I am a student at Chalmers University of Technology in Gothenburg, Sweden. My academic studies have included diverse areas such as user experience, user interfaces, industrial design, product development, programming, and proficiency in Adobe Suite and Figma, along with the foundational engineering courses required like advanced mathematics and physics. I hold a Bachelor's degree of Industrial Design Engineering and am currently in the final year pursuing a Master's of Interaction Design and Technologies.

While I was born in the United States, I have lived and spent all my childhood entirely in Sweden, while later I experienced my teenage years in the multicultural city of Toronto. Holding Swedish, Canadian, and American citizenships, I view this diverse background as a valuable aspect and a great asset for any job or opportunity. Growing up on two different continents has instilled in me a natural instinct to connect, communicate, and build strong bonds. These skills apply daily in professional settings, with friends, and within my family.

My professional experience includes working as a UX Design Intern at Ericsson, where I became an integral part of an agile team, operating within short sprint cycles. During this time, I developed my Figma skills to an expert level and gained practical experience with Confluence and Jira.
